Section C (Theory) Answers:
1. Peaceful Co-existence means different people living together without disagreement or fight. Jesus taught that if someone offends us, we should forgive them and live happily together, thereby promoting peaceful co-existence.
2. Abraham prevented a quarrel between himself and his nephew, Lot. This action demonstrated his desire to maintain peace and avoid conflict, choosing reconciliation over dispute.
3. Jonathan loved David as his own soul. When King Saul planned to kill David, Jonathan saved David by telling him Saul's plans and hiding him. They even made a covenant, showing their deep, selfless bond.
4. Individuals and pupils should show love to one another by sharing belongings, protecting each other in danger, showing love to those in danger, and giving help to the needy.
5. It is important to show unselfish love because Jesus wants us to show love and hospitality to people. We should consider those around us, tell them the truth, and be open-minded, fostering positive relationships and a caring community.
