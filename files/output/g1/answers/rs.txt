Section C (Theory) Answers:
1. Peaceful Co-existence means different people living together without disagreement or fight. It's important because Jesus taught forgiveness and living happily together even after offense, promoting harmony.
2. Esau and Jacob reconciled after Jacob took Esau's blessings, preventing prolonged family strife. Abraham prevented a quarrel between himself and his nephew Lot over their herdsmen, choosing peace over conflict.
3. The moral responsibility is to show unselfish love, considering those around us. Jesus teaches showing love and hospitality, telling the truth, and being open-minded to others.
4. Jonathan loved David as his own soul. When King Saul planned to kill David, Jonathan saved him by revealing Saul's plans and hiding David. Their unselfish love led them to make a covenant.
5. Practical ways include sharing belongings with others, protecting one another when there is danger, and giving help to the needy.
