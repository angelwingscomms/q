Section C (Theory) Answers:
1. Peaceful co-existence means different people living together in peace without any disagreement or fight.
2. Jesus taught us to live peacefully with others and to forgive those who offend us, which helps in maintaining harmonious relationships.
3. Jonathan loved David as his own soul. He demonstrated unselfishness by protecting David from his father King Saul's plans to kill him, hiding him, and telling him Saul's intentions, even though David was a potential rival for the throne. They also made a covenant.
4. Esau and Jacob: Reconciled after Jacob took Esau's blessings, choosing peace over continued animosity. Abraham and Lot: Abraham prevented a quarrel over land by suggesting they separate and allowing Lot to choose first, prioritizing peace over personal gain.
5. Pupils can demonstrate love and unselfishness by sharing their belongings with others, protecting one another when there is danger, telling the truth, being open-minded, and helping the needy.
