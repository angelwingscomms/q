Section C (Theory) Answers:
1. Food is essential for human growth and maintaining health, as it provides necessary nutrients in both liquid and solid forms.
2. Energy-giving food (e.g., yam, maize, cassava) provides energy. Body-building food (e.g., beans, eggs, fish, meat) supplies protein for body development and repair.
3. A balanced diet contains all classes of food (Carbohydrate, protein, vitamins, and minerals) in the right amounts. It makes bones and teeth strong and provides energy and strength.
4. Regular bathing is crucial for personal hygiene to ensure cleanliness, neatness, pleasant body odor, and freedom from body odor. It should be done upon waking, before bed, and after exercises or working.
5. Essential materials for effective bathing include toilet soap for cleansing, a sponge for scrubbing, clean water for washing, and a bucket for holding water.
