Section C (Theory) Answers:
1. God's omnipresence, omniscience, and omnipotence mean He is universally present in person, understanding, and power. For believers, His Holy Spirit indwells them (God the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit). It means we are never separated from His love, can accomplish His will, and our fear, worry, and dissatisfaction are overcome.
