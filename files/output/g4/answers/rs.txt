Section B (Short Answer) Answers:
1. Jesus showed love by healing the sick, delivering the oppressed, opening blind eyes, healing diseases, and raising the dead.
2. Jesus demonstrated love to Blind Bartimaeus, Zacchaeus, and Mary Magdalene.
3. Showing love brings peace, draws us closer to God, brings unity, and brings joy and happiness.
4. The Holy Spirit represents the spirit of Jesus and of God the Father, sent as a counselor and guide.
5. Jesus promised the Holy Spirit because he would soon return to his Father and would not leave them alone; the Spirit would be their counselor.
6. The Holy Spirit would be a counselor, comforter, teacher, guide, reminder, the spirit of truth and prophecy; it would teach righteousness and condemn evil.
7. A new life means a changed life where a person gives up sinful ways, and old character/attitudes are replaced by new ones like truth-telling, love, obedience, and kindness. It comes through repentance and accepting Jesus Christ.
8. Evidence of a new life is seen in a person's character, attitude, and behavior, showing virtues like joy, kindness, and peace.
9. Love, joy (other options include peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, self-control).
10. Obedience, right-living, kindness (other options include being honest, speaking the truth, self-control, gentleness, showing love, forgiving one another).
11. We pray to receive power, strength, and grace to fulfill our mission, to be a role model, to honor God, to build confidence in God, and to receive answers to our needs.
12. Giving thanks to God, confessing sins, forgiving offenders, asking for needs (wisdom, health, government concern), and praying in Jesus' name.
