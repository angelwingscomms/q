Section C (Theory) Answers:
1. Pentecost was a festival observed fifty days after Passover to celebrate the close of spring harvest, marking the completion of the wheat harvest.
2. Jesus fulfilled His promise of sending a comforter; Disciples received the Holy Spirit and started speaking in tongues.
3. A sound like a mighty wind filled the room; Cloven tongues of fire appeared and settled on each disciple.
4. The Holy Spirit guides our utterances while spreading the good news; The Holy Spirit encourages us to keep the gospel.
5. Truth is that which is consistent with the mind, will, character, glory, and being of God.
